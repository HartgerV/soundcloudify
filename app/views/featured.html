Play music EveryWhere. Explore music with EveryOne

Every is a music player that makes listening to music a shared experience. Set up a listening room and invite your friends. Control the playlist together from any device. Let your partyguests decide what to listen and never worry about your music again. Sync music between you and your online friends, explore music together!

Every is currently in a very early stage of development. Most functionality is not yet implemented. Every is created by Hartger Veeman.
